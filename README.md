# Autentico

Practice Spanish through real conversations with an AI. Pick a scenario, choose your role, and chat. The AI stays in character and only speaks Spanish.

## How it works

Choose from 6 built-in scenarios (restaurant, doctor, market, and more) or write your own. The AI plays the other role in the conversation - waiter, doctor, vendor, etc. - and responds naturally in Spanish based on the difficulty you set.

A completion meter tracks how well you're doing as the conversation progresses.

## Scenarios

- At the Restaurant - order food and talk with the waiter
- Planning a Party - organize a birthday with a friend
- At the Doctor - describe symptoms and get a diagnosis
- At the Hotel - check in and ask about services
- At the Market - buy produce and negotiate prices
- Asking for Directions - navigate with a local

You can also write a fully custom scenario with any roles you want.

## Difficulty

Easy - basic vocabulary, patient AI, short responses, yes/no answers accepted

Medium - intermediate vocabulary, some guidance when you're stuck, fuller replies expected

Hard - natural conversation, no help, advanced vocabulary, detailed responses required

You can also fine-tune error tolerance, AI guidance, vocabulary level, response detail, and conversation length individually using the sliders in the sidebar.

## Role swap

Once you start a conversation, you can swap roles - become the waiter instead of the customer, the doctor instead of the patient. Click the role button in the sidebar to switch.
